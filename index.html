Frequently Asked Questions

Can I rent a dumpster for just one day?

Yes, short-term rentals are available for quick cleanups.

What items are prohibited in your dumpsters?

Hazardous waste, flammable materials, tires, and certain electronics are not allowed. Contact us for a complete list.

How does the payment process work? Do I pay upfront or after?

To reserve your dumpster and schedule your delivery, we require payment at the time of booking. We accept all major credit cards for a simple and secure transaction.

I’m a contractor. Can I set up a commercial account with you?

Yes, absolutely. We specialize in building long-term partnerships with contractors. A commercial account can streamline your ordering process, offer flexible billing options, and ensure you have priority service for all your job sites.

How long can I keep the dumpster?

Rental periods vary, but most customers keep them for 7 days. Extensions are available upon request.

How much weight can a dumpster hold?

It depends on the size our team will provide exact weight limits when booking.
